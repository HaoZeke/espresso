Detailed Description

Routines to calculate the generalized lennard jones energy and/or force for a particle pair.

"Generalized" here means that the LJ energy is of the form

eps * [ b1 * (sigma/(r-r_offset))^a1 - b2 * (sigma/(r-r_offset))^a2 + shift]
