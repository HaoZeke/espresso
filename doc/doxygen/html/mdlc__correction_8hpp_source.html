21 /** \file p3m.hpp main header-file for MDLC (magnetic dipolar layer correction).
22  *
23  * Developer: Joan J. Cerda.
24  * Purpose: get the corrections for dipolar 3D algorithms
25  * when applied to a slab geometry and dipolar
26  * particles. DLC & co
27  * Article: A. Brodka, Chemical Physics Letters 400, 62-67 (2004).

29  * We also include a tuning function that returns the
30  * cut-off necessary to attend a certain accuracy.
31  *
32  * Restrictions: the slab must be such that the z is the short

34  *
35  * Limitations: at this moment it is restricted to work with 1 cpu
36  */
