103 /* Constants enumerating the links of a node in the link flux system EK_LINK_xyz
104  is the number of the link in direction (x, y, z), where x, y and z can be 0,
105  U or D representing 0 and one agrid in direction of or against the x, y or z
106  axis. The numbering differs from the one used in the LB since the LB
107  velocities are directed but the links are not. Links 0 - 8 represent
108  the odd LB velocities and numbers 13 - 21 represent the even LB velocities
109  (without the 0). In between there are the links connecting the corners, which
110  represent the 3rd shell not used in the LB but in the advection. The
111  following 13 constants are only defined for the sake of completeness.*/
