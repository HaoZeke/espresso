23 /** \file fft.hpp
24  *
25  * Routines, row decomposition, data structures and communication for the 3D-FFT.
26  *
27  * The 3D-FFT is split into 3 ond dimensional FFTs. The data is
28  * distributed in such a way, that for the actual direction of the
29  * FFT each node has a certain number of rows for which it performs a
30  * 1D-FFT. After performing the FFT on theat direction the data is
31  * redistributed.
32  *
33  * For simplicity at the moment I have implemented a full complex to
34  * complex FFT (even though a real to complex FFT would be
35  * sufficient)
36  *
37  * \todo Combine the forward and backward structures.
38  * \todo The packing routines could be moved to utils.hpp when they are needed elsewhere.
39  *
40  * For more information about FFT usage, see \ref fft.cpp "fft.c".
41 */
