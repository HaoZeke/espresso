Detailed Description

Routines to calculate a flat potential with cosine tail energy and/or force for a particle pair.

Cosine tail is different from that in ljcos.hpp Used for attractive tail/tail interactions in lipid bilayer calculations. Same potential as ljcos2 without Lennard-Jones part. forces.cpp
